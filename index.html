What We Do

Expert Solutions for
Every Garage Door Need

Garage Door Installation

Full-service installation of premium residential and commercial garage doors — from custom wood carriage styles to insulated steel, tailored to your home.

Spring & Cable Replacement

Broken torsion spring or frayed cable? Our certified technicians handle high-tension component replacement safely and efficiently — same day service available.

Emergency Services

24/7 rapid-response for garage door emergencies. Stuck door, off-track panels, or security concerns — we're there when it matters most, day or night.

Opener Installation & Repair

Smart opener upgrades with Wi-Fi, battery backup, and whisper-quiet belt-drive systems. We install, program, and repair all major opener brands.

Panel Replacement

Dented, cracked, or weather-worn panels? We source exact-match replacements to restore your door's strength and curb appeal — without a full replacement.

Weather Sealing

Comprehensive weather seal replacement and insulation upgrades. Keep the elements out, energy costs down, and your garage protected year-round.
About Us

20 Years of Passion
& Precision

For over two decades, Majestic Garage Doors has been more than just a service company—we're your neighbors, dedicated to keeping your home safe, secure, and looking its best. We know a broken garage door is a disruption to your daily life. That's why our team brings a personal touch and uncompromising care to every project.

Whether you need a rapid, reliable repair or are ready to upgrade to a stunning modern door that transforms your curb appeal, we handle it all with precision. When you choose Majestic, you're choosing peace of mind, built on 20 years of trust, craftsmanship, and a genuine commitment to our community.
